## About

I am a high school student interested in experimental physics, particle detection, fusion diagnostics, and the philosophy of science.

My research spans liquid xenon detectors for medical imaging, millimeter-wave diagnostics for fusion plasmas, and particle physics instrumentation.

---

## Research Highlights

### Liquid Xenon PET Detector

First-author publication in JINST on a novel liquid xenon time projection chamber concept for medical imaging.

### Fusion Plasma Diagnostics

Development of microwave diagnostic instrumentation including a 170 GHz waveguide notch filter.

### Dark Matter Detection

Current work focuses on machine-learning approaches for distinguishing WIMP and neutrino events in liquid xenon detectors.
